# Privacy Policy for FocusLah

**Effective Date:** January 8, 2026

## Introduction

FocusLah ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Local Data Only

FocusLah stores all user data locally on your device. We collect and store:

- **Task Data**: Tasks you create, including titles, completion status, and priority levels
- **Timer Data**: Your Pomodoro timer history and completed session count
- **App Preferences**: Your app settings and preferences

### Network Activity

The app uses internet connectivity for the following features:

- **RSS Feed Fetching**: The app downloads motivational quotes and productivity articles from public RSS feeds
- These RSS feeds are fetched from third-party websites (like Zen Habits, BrainyQuote)
- No personal data is sent to these websites
- The app only reads publicly available content

### Information We DO NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect any personal information
- We do NOT collect your name, email, or contact information
- We do NOT track your location
- We do NOT collect device identifiers for tracking purposes
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking tools
- We do NOT share any data with third parties
- We do NOT send any of your tasks, timer data, or personal information over the internet

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your Android device using:
- SharedPreferences for user settings
- Local storage for task and timer data

Your data never leaves your device.

## Data Backup

If you enable Android's auto-backup feature in your device settings, your app data may be backed up to your Google Drive account. This is controlled by your device's system settings, not by our app.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is suitable for all ages and contains no inappropriate content.

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

- **INTERNET**: To fetch motivational quotes and productivity articles from RSS feeds
- **ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE**: To check if internet is available before attempting to fetch RSS feeds
- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS**: To send you notifications when timer sessions complete (optional - you can disable notifications in device settings)

### Offline Functionality

- The app works completely offline for core features (tasks, timer, local quotes)
- Internet is only required for fetching fresh RSS feed content
- If no internet is available, the app uses locally stored quotes

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:
1. Going to your device Settings
2. Apps → FocusLah
3. Storage → Clear Data

Or by uninstalling the app.

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect, store, or process any personal data on our servers, there is no data to access, modify, or delete from our end. All your data remains on your device under your control.

## Third-Party Services

### RSS Feed Sources

The app fetches content from public RSS feeds, including:
- BrainyQuote (motivational quotes)
- Zen Habits (productivity articles)
- Lifehack (productivity tips)
- Pick the Brain (self-improvement content)

These are publicly available RSS feeds. When the app fetches content:
- No personal data is transmitted
- No tracking cookies are used
- Only publicly available content is retrieved
- The app acts as a standard RSS reader

### No Analytics or Tracking

- The app does NOT use Google Analytics
- The app does NOT use Firebase Analytics
- The app does NOT use any crash reporting services
- The app does NOT use any advertising SDKs
- The app does NOT track your behavior

## Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend using a secure screen lock on your device.

**Summary**: FocusLah is a privacy-focused productivity app that stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.
